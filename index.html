I'm Offred

WHO AM I

I am a handmaid. I am one of the remaining fertile women in Gilead whose job is to produce viable offspring for the Commander and his Wife. I live in the Republic of Gilead which replaces what was once the United States of America.

BEFORE

A long time ago, I was once married to a man named Luke. I loved him dearly, and I also once had a little girl. I lived a happy life, until I was robbed of my job and my property like all other women of the country. Luke, my daughter, and I decided to flee to Canada, but we were captured and have never seen each other ever since. I was then sent to a Red Center where I was taught lessons by a woman named Aunt Lydia about women's role as an obedient child bearer and becoming a handmaid.

LIFE IN THE COMMANDER'S HOUSE

I was finally assigned to the Commander's house to bear children for Serena Joy, his wife. Gradually, a routine formed. I would take shopping trips with Ofglen, my friend, and visit the doctor to check for diseases. Every month, I would have ritualized sexual intercourse with the Commander to try and have a baby.

Later in the night, I had sexual intercourse with the Commander.
