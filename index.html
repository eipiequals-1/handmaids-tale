I'm Offred

WHO AM I

I am a handmaid. I am one of the remaining fertile women in Gilead whose job is to produce viable offspring for the Commander and his Wife. I live in the Republic of Gilead which replaces what was once the United States of America.

BEFORE

A few years ago, before the revolution, I was married to a man named Luke. I loved him dearly, and I also had a little girl. I lived a happy life, until I was robbed of my job and my property like all other women of the country. Luke, my daughter, and I decided to flee to Canada, but we were captured on the way and have never seen each other ever since. I was then sent to a Red Center where I was taught lessons by a woman named Aunt Lydia about women's role as an obedient child bearer and becoming a handmaid.

Now, I have to wear a red dress with a white veil. I do not have any choice. I can not visit men, and I can only visit women of a lower social status.

LIFE IN THE COMMANDER'S HOUSE

I was finally assigned to the Commander's house to bear children for Serena Joy, his wife. Gradually, a routine formed. I would take shopping trips with Ofglen, my handmaid friend, and visit the doctor to check for diseases. Every month, I would have ritualized sexual intercourse with the Commander to try and have a baby.

Before I left, he asked me for a kiss, to which I reluctantly agreed. The visits to his office became more frequent and I grew closer to him. After several months where I did not become pregnant, Serena Joy asked me to be impregnated by Nick in return for a picture of my daughter. I realized then, that Serena had always known where my daughter was. On the same night, the Commander smuggled me out to an illegal nightclub with prostitutes. I met my old friend from the Red Center, Moira, who had become one of the women at the nightclub. Later in the night, I was forced to have sexual intercourse with the Commander.
