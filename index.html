- WHAT I DO -

HANDMAID OF GILEAD

I am a handmaid in the society of Gilead, the remains of the United States of America after the second Civil War.

JOB WITH COMMANDER

I work for the respectable Commander, his wife Serena Joy, and other servants in his house. My role is to produce offspring for the couple.

RESPECTED WOMAN

I am a respected woman of Gilead since the number of fertile women is diminishing. I get everything for free from the Commander.
