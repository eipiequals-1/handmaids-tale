BIO

I'm Offred

WHO AM I

I am a handmaid of the Republic of Gilead which was once the east coast of the United States of America. My job in Gilead is to produce viable offspring for couples.

BEFORE

A few years ago, before the revolution, I was married to a man named Luke. I loved him dearly, and I also had a little girl. I lived a happy life, until I was robbed of my job and my property like all other women of the country. Luke, my daughter, and I decided to flee to Canada, but we were captured on the way and have never seen each other ever since. I was then sent to a Red Center where I was taught lessons by a woman named Aunt Lydia about women's role as an obedient child bearer and becoming a handmaid.

Now, I have to wear a red dress with a white veil. I do not have any choice. I can not go out in public alone.

LIFE IN THE COMMANDER'S HOUSE

I was finally assigned to the Commander's house to bear children for Serena Joy, his wife. Gradually, a routine formed. I would take shopping trips with Ofglen, my handmaid friend, and visit the doctor.

Every month, I would have try to be impregnated by the Commander and have a baby. Unfortunately for me, it was never successful.
